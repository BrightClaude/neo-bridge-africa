FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Neo Bridge Africa shipping.

Will I get PV for my products?
Yes. Each product's PV is added to your PV for that month. Your accumulated PV is fully credited regardless of where the products are shipped from.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery takes 10 to 14 days from the batch dispatch date. You will be notified once your batch is shipped and when your order arrives in Nigeria.
Does my PV accumulation determine my shipment fee?
Yes. Your accumulated PV determines your shipping fee. The fee ranges between ₦30,000 and ₦50,000 depending on your total PV for that shipment cycle.
